About S & J Foods

a journey in taste

Here at S & J Foods we love to eat and we enjoy sharing our love for food with our clients☺. How we feed you is how we would eat... great homely recipes that are presented with great elegance and sophistication.

We also produce cakes for all occasions, we do catering for all types of events and we create themed sweet and dessert tables- almost like a one stop shop for good grub. We work with you to make your event that memorable and special, hence our slogan: ‘We are your personal chefs’ -and we stick by it☺.

All the products made are done so in- house and made with the freshest ingredientsand of the finest quality possible. Please be aware that one is not limited to the variety of choices you see online; if you do not find what you are looking for please do contact us for further information. We are more than happy to help.

event catering

S & J Foods provide private catering for weddings, birthdays, christenings and private parties. We also do corporate events including light refreshments at meetings, hot lunch buffets and afternoon tea snacks.

We like to work with clients rather than have a set menu. This allows us to create a menu more tailored to you and your momentous events. We also can arrange crockery, cutlery, glassware and linenif required.

Sweet and dessert table

Sweet and Desert Tables are very trendy and add great class and elegance to any event. These can be themed to complement your occasion, be it colour coordination or style formation.

Dessert Tables require a bit more organisation and planning, and as such there are no set menus. The menu chioce here will need to complement the themes set fourth by the event's main menu, so S & J Foods will work with you to reach an elegantly-tailored outcome.
